Privacy Policy

Last updated: 22 July 2026

This page explains how Sajilo Quiz at sajiloquiz.vercel.app handles information. The app is a free quiz presentation tool built and maintained by Arun Neupane. In short: your quiz content lives on your own device, and we do not collect or store your personal data on any server.

What stays on your device

  • Questions and rounds: everything you create in the admin panel is saved in your browser's local storage on your device. It is never uploaded to us.
  • Scores and progress: team scores and completed questions are also stored locally, and the reset button clears them.
  • Offline use: if you install the app, your browser caches the app files so it runs without internet. No personal data is involved in that process.

Cookies and local storage

Sajilo Quiz itself does not set tracking cookies. It uses local storage purely so your questions and scores survive a page refresh. Third parties described below may set cookies when ads are shown.

  • You may opt out of personalised advertising at Google Ads Settings, and opt out of some third-party vendors' cookies at aboutads.info.

Children's privacy

Sajilo Quiz is a general-audience tool often used in schools by teachers and organisers. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, children included.
